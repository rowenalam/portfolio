studEbud

Overview

People of all ages and backgrounds take courses and are often learning new things. studEbud is a mobile app I created to be your pocket study buddy! studEbud is a fun and easy way for users to learn vocabulary on-the-go in quick 5-10 minute study sessions.

Purpose and Context

This was a project I completed during my UX Fundamentals course at CareerFoundry. Designing this app helped us to practice the design thinking process while learning new vocabulary along the way.

Role

UX Researcher & UX Designer

Duration

The course project was designed to take 1 month if working at a rate of 15 hours per week, but it took me 2 months to complete. It has been a long time since I have been in school and I had to get accustomed to working full-time and studying on the side. I also had to retrain myself on how to study since the course content was very important for me to advance my career.

Objective

To empower people to learn new vocabulary.
