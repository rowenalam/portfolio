Fitted

Overview

Getting into an exercise routine can be difficult and intimidating. Fitted is a web app that allows users to easily fit exercise into their busy schedules and workout in any setting, even without equipment. There are 5 minute to hour long activities with guide to show the correct form.

Purpose and Context

This webapp was a project I completed during my UI for UX Designers course at CareerFoundry. We practiced the mobile-first design approach while learning about breakpoints and responsive design.

Role

UI Designer

Duration

The course project was designed to take 2 months if working at a rate of 15 hours per week. It was completed during 3.5 months between Nov 8, 2019 - Feb 18, 2020: consisting of 6 weeks of actual work and 9 weeks of holiday breaks.

Objective

Motivate people into an exercise routine that suits their level, schedule, and interests.

User Flow

The purpose of my course was to focus on UI Design, so the UX portion with research and personas were given to us in a project brief. I listed the specific tasks I needed to accomplish with my web app.

Wireframes

To practice mobile-first design, I began by sketching out only the mobile screens in pen. I used Marvel to make a low-fidelity prototype to test out the flow of the screens. Then, I used AdobeXD to create the screens in mid-fidelity.

Style Guide

With the mood board in mind, I added the UI elements to Fitted. This led to my Hi-Fidelity prototype that you can play with at the end.

Responsive Design

Up until now, we were only focused on mobile. However, the point of a web app is for user to be able to access the content on all devices. I sketched out how the app would look like on tablet and desktop. Next, I used a 12-column grid to find breakpoints to have the perfect view on all platforms. And finally, we have our mockup!
