About

My Story

My name is Rowena and I was born and raised in the Bay Area, California. Ever since I was young, I loved art but I never thought to pursue it as a career. Instead, I grew up tutoring younger students and went to school to become a teacher, leading to my background in Child Development.

I found myself feeling unchallenged and unfulfilled, so I explored other fields such as human resources, wedding planning and somehow ended up being a fraud analyst. In 2017, I finally discovered the growing field of User Experience and decided to change my path to become a UX/UI Designer.
